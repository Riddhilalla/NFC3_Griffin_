Frequently Asked Questions

Here you'll find answers to common questions about our rehabilitation services and how we can support your journey to recovery

What types of therapies do you offer?

We offer a range of therapies including individual counseling, group therapy, cognitive-behavioral therapy (CBT), and family therapy. Our goal is to provide personalized care tailored to your needs.

How can I get started with your rehabilitation programs?

To begin, you can schedule an initial consultation with one of our specialists. During this meeting, we'll assess your needs and create a personalized treatment plan to support your recovery journey.

Do you offer support for family members?

Yes, we provide family support services including counseling and education to help loved ones understand and support the recovery process. We believe family involvement is crucial to successful rehabilitation.

Are your services covered by insurance?

Many of our services are covered by insurance. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to verify coverage details and discuss any out-of-pocket costs that may apply.

What if I need help outside of regular office hours?

We offer emergency support and crisis intervention for those in need of immediate assistance. Please contact our emergency hotline or reach out to your case manager for guidance outside of regular office hours.

Can I choose a specific therapist or doctor?

Yes, you can request to work with a specific therapist or doctor based on your preferences and needs. We will do our best to accommodate your request and match you with a specialist who is best suited to your treatment goals.
